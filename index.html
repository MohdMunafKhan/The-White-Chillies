About Us

Welcome to "The White Chillies", where great food and a cozy vibe come together. At "The White Chillies", we invite you to savor a unique dining experience where modern elegance meets culinary artistry. Our restaurant has a warm, welcoming feel with earthy colors, soft lights, and a lovely outdoor patio, perfect for relaxed meals or fun gatherings, seating up to 80 indoors and 30 outside, plus a private room for 15. Our diverse menu celebrates both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, from creamy Paneer Tikka Masala and spicy Vegetable Vindaloo to succulent Butter Chicken and tender Lamb Rogan Josh, alongside international favorites like Italian Truffle Risotto, Thai Green Curry, and Mexican Chicken Enchiladas, all crafted with fresh, local ingredients. Our talented Chef and his team mix tradition with new ideas, while our dedicated staff ensures every visit is memorable. Open daily from 11 AM to 11 PM, The White Chilies is the place for delicious food and good times that delights the senses.
